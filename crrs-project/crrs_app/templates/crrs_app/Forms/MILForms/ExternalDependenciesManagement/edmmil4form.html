1. Are external dependency management activities periodically reviewed and measured to ensure they are effective and producing intended results? [EXD:GG2.GP8 & GP9],[GG2.GP8 & GP9]

To ensure the external dependency management activities (process) remain effective and produce intended results by periodic review and measurement.

Criteria for “Yes” Response: • All external dependency management activities are periodically (as defined by the organization) reviewed and measured and the results evaluated.

Criteria for “Incomplete” Response: • The organization has not established a frequency for review of the external dependency management activities. • Or; review and measurement address some of the external dependency management activities. • Or; external dependency management activities are reviewed but not measured.
