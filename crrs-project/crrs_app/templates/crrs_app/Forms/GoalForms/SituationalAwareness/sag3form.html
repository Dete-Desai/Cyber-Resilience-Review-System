1. Is threat information communicated to stakeholders? [COMM:SG3.SP2]
Read More

To determine if threat information is communicated to all identified internal and external stakeholders.

Criteria for “Yes” Response: • Threat information is communicated to all identified stakeholders (established in SA:G2.Q1 and SA:G2.Q2) as required.

Criteria for “Incomplete” Response: • Threat information is communicated to some of the identified stakeholders. • Or; some threat information is communicated to all identified stakeholders.

2. Have resources been assigned authority and accountability for communicating threat information? [COMM:SG2.SP3]
Read More

To determine if the authority and accountability for communicating threat information has been assigned.

Criteria for “Yes” Response: • The authority and accountability for communicating threat information has been assigned to all responsible resources.

Criteria for “Incomplete” Response: • The authority and accountability for communicating threat information has been assigned to some of the responsible resources.

3. Have resources been trained with respect to their specific role in communicating threat information? [COMM:SG2.SP3]
Read More

To determine if the resources responsible for communicating threat information have been trained for their specific role.

Criteria for “Yes” Response: • All resources assigned to perform threat communication activities have been trained.

Criteria for “Incomplete” Response: • Some resources assigned to perform threat communication activities have been trained.
